The 70s

The 70's were an incerdible decade for music as a whole, and an incerdible decade for David Bowie.
In 1970 he realesed the album "The Man Who Sold The world", and in 1972, he released the "The Rise and Fall of Ziggy Stardust and the Spiders from Mars", which was a revolutionary album, that had many hits, the most succesful of which was "Starman".
In this era he also went on a tour, which solidified him as a major rock music figure. in this decade he also released the albums "Station To station" which had a bit of darker vibe.

Bowie collaborated with several artists, and he recorded the single "Fame" with John Lennon whom he met in New York. this single made it to the top of the U.S charts and was his first U.S #1 song.
